LỚP 1: GIAO TIẾP HÀNG NGÀY (Daily Communication)
1.Good morning / Good afternoon (Chào buổi sáng / Chào buổi chiều)
①Good morning, how are you today?
②Good afternoon, nice to meet you.
2.Thank you very much (Cảm ơn bạn rất nhiều)
①Thank you very much for your help.
②Thank you very much for the coffee.
3.You're welcome (Không có chi / Chào mừng bạn)
①"Thank you for the food." - "You're welcome."
②You're welcome to join our team.
4.Excuse me (Xin lỗi cho tôi hỏi / Xin nhường đường)
①Excuse me, where is the bathroom?
②Excuse me, can I ask a question?
5.I'm sorry (Tôi xin lỗi)
①I'm sorry, I am late for the meeting.
②I'm sorry, I don't understand.
LỚP 2: THÔNG TIN CÁ NHÂN (Personal Information)
6.My name is... (Tên tôi là...)
①My name is Nam, and I am a developer.
②My name is John, nice to meet you.
7.I am from... (Tôi đến từ...)
①I am from Vietnam, and I live in Hanoi.
②I am from Japan, but I work in the US.
8.I am [number] years old (Tôi [số] tuổi)
①I am 25 years old.
②I am 30 years old this year.
9.I live in... (Tôi sống ở...)
①I live in Ho Chi Minh City with my family.
②I live in a small apartment.
10.My hobby is... / I like... (Sở thích của tôi là... / Tôi thích...)
①My hobby is playing video games.
②I like listening to music in my free time.
LỚP 3: ĐỜI SỐNG & SINH HOẠT (Everyday Life)
11.Wake up / Get up (Thức dậy / Rời khỏi giường)
①I wake up at 6 AM every day.
②What time do you get up on Sunday?
12.Go to work / Go to school (Đi làm / Đi học)
①I go to work by motorbike.
②Children go to school from Monday to Friday.
13.Have breakfast / lunch / dinner (Ăn sáng / trưa / tối)
①I have breakfast at 7 AM.
②Let's have lunch together today.
14.Go home (Đi về nhà)
①I go home at 6 PM after work.
②I want to go home and rest.
15.Go to bed (Đi ngủ)
①I usually go to bed at 11 PM.
②Children should go to bed early.
LỚP 4: TRONG CÔNG VIỆC CƠ BẢN (Basic Work)
16.I am a... [Job] (Tôi là một...)
①I am a software engineer.
②I am a student at IT university.
17.Work from home (Làm việc tại nhà)
①I work from home every Friday.
②Do you like to work from home?
18.Check email (Kiểm tra hòm thư điện tử)
①I check my email every morning.
②Please check your email for the ticket.
19.Open the file / Close the window (Mở file / Đóng cửa sổ)
①Please open the file on your computer.
②Can you close the window, please?
20.Have a question (Có một câu hỏi)
①I have a question about this project.
②Do you have any questions for me?
