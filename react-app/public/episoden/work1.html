LỚP 1: THẢO LUẬN & ĐỒNG BỘ TIẾN ĐỘ (Status & Alignment)
1.Give an update on... (Cập nhật tình hình về...)
①Could you give us a update on the backend development progress?
②I will give a quick update on the system migration during the meeting.
2.Schedule a meeting (Lên lịch một cuộc họp)
①Let's schedule a meeting for tomorrow afternoon to discuss the tech stack.
②I need to schedule a brief meeting with the frontend team to sync up.
3.Move the deadline (Dời hạn chót / Thay đổi thời hạn)
①We had to move the deadline to next Friday because of some unexpected issues.
②Is it possible to move the deadline back by two days?
4.Keep things on track (Giữ cho mọi thứ đúng tiến độ/kế hoạch)
①Daily standup meetings help us keep things on track.
②We need to work hard this week to keep the project on track.
5.Fall behind schedule (Bị chậm trễ so với kế hoạch/tiến độ)
①If we don't fix this bug today, we will fall behind schedule.
②The team is working overtime because we fell behind schedule last week.
LỚP 2: LÀM VIỆC NHÓM & PHÂN CÔNG (Collaboration & Tasks)
6.Assign a task to... (Giao một nhiệm vụ/task cho ai đó)
①The project manager will assign this task to the senior developer.
②I was assigned a task to optimize the database queries yesterday.
7.Take responsibility for... (Chịu trách nhiệm cho...)
①I will take responsibility for setting up the CI/CD pipeline.
②Who is going to take responsibility for reviewing the source code?
8.Work as a team (Làm việc như một đội / Phối hợp nhóm)
①We need to work as a team to solve this complex system error.
②I love this project because everyone works so well as a team.
9.Share the workload (Chia sẻ khối lượng công việc)
①We should hire another engineer to share the workload.
②Thank you for helping me share the workload during this busy sprint.
10.Reach a consensus (Đạt được sự đồng thuận/thống nhất ý kiến)
①After a long discussion, the team finally reached a consensus on the architecture.
②It's important to reach a consensus before writing the final code.
LỚP 3: GIẢI QUYẾT VẤN ĐỀ & ĐỀ XUẤT (Problem Solving & Proposals)
11.Point out... (Chỉ ra, vạch ra điều gì)
①During the code review, he pointed out a potential memory leak.
②I want to point out a small mistake in the API documentation.
12.Address an issue (Giải quyết, xử lý một vấn đề)
①We must address this security issue before deploying to production.
②The new software patch will address the connection timeout issue.
13.Propose a solution (Đề xuất một giải pháp)
①I would like to propose a solution to improve database performance.
②He proposed a great solution for handling large file uploads.
14.Take actions (Hành động, bắt tay vào làm để giải quyết việc gì)
①We need to take immediate actions to recover the server.
②The management team decided to take actions to improve code quality.
15.Put on hold (Tạm dừng, hoãn lại một kế hoạch/dự án)
①The new feature has been put on hold until next month.
②We had to put the trading bot project on hold to focus on core services.
LỚP 4: ĐÁNH GIÁ & KẾT QUẢ (Evaluation & Outcomes)
16.Meet expectations (Đáp ứng/đạt được kỳ vọng)
①The performance of the new FastAPI microservice meets our expectations.
②We hope the new system architecture will meet the client's expectations.
17.Evaluate performance (Đánh giá hiệu suất/hiệu năng/kết quả công việc)
①The tech lead will evaluate the system's performance under high traffic.
②Every six months, the company will evaluate our performance for a salary review.
18.Provide feedback (Đưa ra phản hồi, nhận xét)
①Please provide your feedback on the new user interface design.
②The QA engineer provided clear feedback on the bugs they found.
19.Make an adjustments (Điều chỉnh, sửa đổi một chút cho phù hợp)
①We need to make a slight adjustment to the database configuration.
②After testing, we made some adjustments to the background job scheduler.
20.Deliver results (Mang lại kết quả, bàn giao kết quả công việc)
①The development team worked hard to deliver the results on time.
②In this role, you are expected to deliver high-quality results every sprint.
