1.Stick to a routine (Kiên trì giữ một thói quen/lịch trình)
①It is hard but rewarding to stick to a routine of exercising every morning.
②If you want to improve your English, you need to stick to a study routine.
2.Squeeze in a workout (Tranh thủ thời gian để tập thể dục)
①Even on busy days, I try to squeeze in a workout like a quick 20-minute run.
②I usually squeeze in a workout in the evening after finishing all my tasks.
3.Clear my mind (Thư giãn đầu óc, giải tỏa suy nghĩ)
①Going for a long walk in the park helps me clear my mind after a stressful day.
②I need a short break right now just to clear my mind and refocus.
4.Keep track of expenses (Theo dõi, ghi chép các khoản chi tiêu)
①Using a mobile app makes it much easier to keep track of monthly expenses.
②We should keep track of our expenses to save up for a new house and a car.
5.Stay up late (Thức khuya)
①I used to stay up late to finish my work, but now I prefer waking up early.
②If you stay up late too often, it will negatively affect your health.
LỚP 2: GIAO TIẾP & MỐI QUAN HỆ (Communication & Relationships)
6.Get straight to the point (Đi thẳng vào vấn đề)
①Please get straight to the point because we don't have much time left.
②I like the way he explains things; he always gets straight to the point.
7.Keep someone informed (Cập nhật tình hình/thông tin cho ai đó kịp thời)
①Please keep me informed if there are any changes to the plan.
②I will keep my family informed about the schedule of our upcoming trip.
8.Be on the same page (Có cùng ý kiến, hiểu nhau, đồng lòng)
①Before we start the discussion, let's make sure we are all on the same page.
②It's great to work with a partner when both of you are on the same page.
9.Share the financial burden (Chia sẻ gánh nặng tài chính/kinh tế)
①Working hard allows me to share the financial burden with my family.
②They support each other to share the financial burden of raising their children.
10.Give someone a hand (Giúp đỡ ai đó một tay)
①Could you give me a hand with these heavy boxes, please?
②I am always ready to give my colleagues a hand when they are overloaded.
LỚP 3: CÔNG VIỆC & QUẢN LÝ THỜI GIAN (Work & Time Management)
11.Meet a tight deadline (Kịp hoàn thành công việc trong một thời hạn rất gấp)
①The whole team had to work overtime last week to meet a tight deadline.
②It's stressful but satisfying when you manage to meet a tight deadline.
12.Make a career transition (Chuyển đổi hướng đi sự nghiệp/chuyển ngành)
①He is preparing his portfolio to make a career transition in the next few months.
②It takes a lot of courage and effort to make a successful career transition.
13.Balance work and life (Cân bằng giữa công việc và cuộc sống)
①Finding a way to balance work and life is essential for long-term happiness.
②It is quite difficult to balance work and life when you have a young child.
14.Take something for granted (Coi điều gì đó là hiển nhiên nên không trân trọng)
①We often take our health for granted until we experience a sickness.
②Don't take your family's support for granted; always show your gratitude.
15.Call it a day (Kết thúc một ngày làm việc/hoạt động)
①We have solved the major bugs, so let's call it a day and go home.
②It's already 9 PM; I think it's time to call it a day.
LỚP 4: RA QUYẾT ĐỊNH & GIẢI QUYẾT VẤN ĐỀ (Decisions & Problem Solving)
16.Weigh the pros and cons (Cân nhắc kỹ mặt lợi và mặt hại/ưu và nhược điểm)
①You should weigh the pros and cons carefully before buying a new house.
②Let's weigh the pros and cons of each solution before making a final choice.
17.Take a calculated risk (Chấp nhận một rủi ro đã có sự tính toán/lường trước)
①Investing in the financial market always requires taking a calculated risk.
②Moving to a new city was a calculated risk, but it completely paid off.
18.Learn it the hard way (Tự rút ra bài học xương máu từ sai lầm/thất bại của bản thân)
①I didn't back up my data and lost everything; I had to learn it the hard way.
②He refused to listen to advice and learned the hard way after losing his money.
19.Come up with a solution (Nghĩ ra một giải pháp để xử lý vấn đề)
①We spent the whole afternoon trying to come up with a solution to the issue.
②She is very creative and always comes up with a great solution to challenges.
20.Put something into practice (Đưa cái gì đó vào thực tế/thực hành)
①Learning new vocabulary is useless if you don't put it into practice.
②I want to put the time management techniques I learned into practice tomorrow.
