LỚP 1: THÓI QUEN & SỨC KHỎE (Daily Habits & Health)
1.Stay in shape (Giữ vóc dáng cân đối, khỏe mạnh)
①Running every morning is a great way to stay in shape.
②I try to eat healthy meals to stay in shape and feel better.
2.Take a quick nap (Chợp mắt một lát, ngủ trưa ngắn)
①I usually take a quick nap for 15 minutes after lunch.
②A quick nap can help you recharge your energy for the afternoon.
3.Get enough sleep (Ngủ đủ giấc)
①It is very important to get enough sleep to stay focused at work.
②I rarely drink coffee in the evening because I want to get enough sleep.
4.Catch a cold (Bị cảm lạnh)
①Remember to wear a jacket, or you will catch a cold.
②I caught a cold last week because of the sudden weather change.
5.Take care of... (Chăm sóc, lo liệu cho...)
①He stays at home to take care of his son on the weekend.
②Don't worry about the project; I will take care of it.
LỚP 2: GIAO TIẾP & ĐỜI SỐNG (Social & Everyday Life)
6.Stay in touch (Giữ liên lạc)
①We don't see each other often, but we still stay in touch.
②Let's stay in touch through Zalo or email after this project.
7.Make a decision (Đưa ra quyết định)
①I need more time to make a decision about buying a new car.
②It is not easy to make a final decision when you have many choices.
8.Change your mind (Thay đổi ý định/quyết định)
①I was going to go out, but I changed my mind because of the rain.
②If you change your mind, please let me know as soon as possible.
9.Waste time / Waste money (Lãng phí thời gian / Lãng phí tiền bạc)
①Don't waste time scrolling on your phone when you are working.
②Buying things you don't use is a huge waste of money.
10.Pay attention to... (Chú ý, để tâm đến điều gì)
①You should pay attention to the security guidelines when opening ports.
②Please pay attention to what the speaker is saying.
LỚP 3: CÔNG VIỆC & THỜI GIAN (Work & Planning)
11.Run out of... (Hết, cạn kiệt cái gì đó)
①We are going to run out of storage space if we don't clean the DB.
②I need to go to the supermarket because we ran out of milk.
12.Make progress (Có tiến bộ, có tiến triển)
①His English is making great progress after two months of practicing.
②We are making progress on fixing the critical bugs of the system.
13.Take your time (Cứ thong thả, không cần vội)
①Take your time to review the code; there is no rush.
②You can take your time to think about my proposal.
14.Keep an eye on... (Để mắt tới, theo dõi sát sao)
①Please keep an eye on the server status while I am away.
②We need to keep an eye on the market trends this week.
15.Figure out... (Tìm ra cách giải quyết, hiểu ra vấn đề)
①Give me ten minutes to figure out why the script failed.
②I finally figured out how to fix the connection timeout issue.
LỚP 4: CẢM XÚC & PHẢN ỨNG (Emotions & Responses)
16.Deal with... (Đối phó, xử lý một vấn đề khó khăn)
①As a backend developer, I have to deal with high traffic every day.
②Learning how to deal with stress is important for everyone.
17.Look forward to... (Mong đợi, trông chờ điều gì)
①I am looking forward to hearing your feedback on my technical design.
②The family is looking forward to the summer vacation.
18.Make a mistake (Phạm sai lầm, mắc lỗi)
①It is okay to make a mistake, as long as you learn from it.
②I made a small mistake in the database configuration yesterday.
19.Get used to... (Quen với điều gì/việc gì)
①It took me a month to get used to the new development environment.
②You will soon get used to speaking English every day.
20.Make sense (Có lý, hợp lý, dễ hiểu)
①His explanation makes a lot of sense; now I understand the logic.
②This error message doesn't make any sense to me.
